# /sprint-plan

> Sprint planning with capacity management and prioritization.

## Routing

Execute this skill by combining:
1. **Agent persona**: `@planning-agent`
2. **Skill instructions**: `skills/planning/sprint-plan.md`
3. **User context**: `agents/_shared/context.md`

## What This Does

Creates a comprehensive sprint plan including:
- Sprint goal
- Capacity calculation with buffer
- Prioritized items (P0/P1/P2)
- Dependencies and risks
- Success criteria

## Quick Use

Just run `/sprint-plan` and provide:
- Team size and sprint length
- Backlog items to consider
- Known dependencies or constraints

The agent applies prioritization frameworks and ensures realistic commitment.
